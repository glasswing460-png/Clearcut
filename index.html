How it works

Clearcut runs an open-source AI segmentation model directly inside your browser using WebAssembly. Your images are processed on your own computer and never leave it — there is no server, no account, and no upload.

The first time you remove a background, the model (~40 MB) downloads once and is cached, so every run after that is fast and works even offline. It's free to use and free to host.
